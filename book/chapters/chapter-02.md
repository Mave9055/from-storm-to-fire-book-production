## Chapter Two – The Rising Fire

Embers danced in the wind, carrying the scent of scorched earth. What the storm had broken, the fire would now consume. It was a cycle as old as time itself.

The village was in chaos. People were running, screaming, trying to save what little they had left. Bret moved through the crowd with a calm that surprised even himself. He had been through worse. He had survived the storm; he would survive the fire.

"Gather at the square!" he shouted, his voice cutting through the noise. "We leave now!"

They looked at him with eyes full of fear and hope. He was their only chance. He was the one who had seen the fire coming. He was the one who knew the way out.

As they began their journey into the unknown, Bret looked back one last time. The village was gone, swallowed by the flames. But they were alive. And as long as they were alive, there was hope.
