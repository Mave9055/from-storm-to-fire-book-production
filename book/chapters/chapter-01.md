## Chapter One – The Gathering Storm

The sky turned a bruised purple, the kind of color that promised nothing but destruction. Lightning arced across the horizon, a jagged gold vein in the heart of the darkness. This was the beginning. From the storm, the fire would rise.

Bret Lingar stood on the edge of the cliff, feeling the wind whip against his face. He had seen many storms in his life, but none like this. This was not just weather; it was a reckoning.

The air was thick with the scent of ozone and salt. Below, the ocean churned, its waves crashing against the rocks with a violence that mirrored the turmoil in his own soul. He had spent years running from the past, but the past had a way of catching up, especially when the world was on fire.

"It's time," he whispered to the wind.

He turned away from the edge and began the long walk back to the village. He knew what he had to do. He had to warn them. He had to lead them. He had to survive.
