# Dark storm/fire ebook style guide

## Overall mood

- Dark, cinematic, moody.
- Feels like standing in a storm with fire on the horizon.
- Professional and readable, not “edgy for the sake of edgy”.

## Colors

- **Background (main pages):**
  - Charcoal / near-black: `#0b0c10` to `#111217`
- **Text (body):**
  - Soft white: `#f5f5f5`
- **Headers / accents:**
  - Gold / fire orange: `#f5a623` or `#ff9f1c`
- **Secondary accent (storm):**
  - Deep desaturated blue/gray: `#1f2833` or `#202733`
- **Borders (chapter openers):**
  - Gold: `#f5a623`

## Typography

- **Body font:**
  - A clean, sans-serif or serif that renders well in PDFs.
  - Examples: "Georgia", "Merriweather", "Source Serif Pro", or "Libre Baskerville".
- **Heading font:**
  - Slightly bolder, can be the same family as body.
  - Use letter-spacing for a cinematic feel.

## Layout

### Chapter opener pages

- **Background:**
  - Dark gradient: charcoal to deep blue.
  - Optional subtle radial gradient behind the title.
- **Border:**
  - Thin gold border around the page content area.
- **Title:**
  - Large, gold/orange text.
  - Centered vertically and horizontally.
  - Optional small subtitle in stormy gray.
- **Accent:**
  - Subtle storm/fire motif:
    - A faint overlay gradient from deep blue to orange.
    - Very low opacity texture (if used) to avoid hurting readability.

### Normal reading pages

- **Background:**
  - Solid charcoal or very subtle gradient.
- **Text:**
  - White or near-white.
  - Comfortable line height (e.g., 1.6).
  - Reasonable line length (max-width for text).
- **Headers:**
  - Gold/orange for chapter titles and section headings.
- **Margins:**
  - Generous margins to avoid edge-to-edge text.

## Accessibility and readability

- Maintain high contrast between text and background.
- Avoid large blocks of all-caps text.
- Keep decorative elements away from the main reading area.
- Use consistent spacing between paragraphs and headings.

## Branding notes

- Use the same gold/orange accent on:
  - Chapter titles
  - Page numbers
  - Small dividers or rules
- Storm/fire is a **mood**, not a distraction:
  - No heavy textures behind body text.
  - Keep the drama on chapter openers and section breaks.
